About Me

I don't really talk much. But when a topic hooks me in, I can go on for hours. Anime, games, movies, music — these are the things that pull me into conversation.

I like to play it slow. Stay low key. I've always been the curious type — the kid who would flip through books about different animals and faraway places on earth just to see what's out there.

I love computers and building software, but this isn't about work. This is just me.

What I Like

Movies & Shows

Drama, sci-fi, action — anything with a great ending or a plottwist that hits hard. Interstellar, The Pianist, Bohemian Rhapsody, The Good Doctor, Band of Brothers, Supernatural, Walking Dead, Generation Kill, IT, From, The Whale. The list never really ends.

Anime

Frieren, Attack on Titan, Chainsaw Man, Heavenly Delusion, The Angel Next Door Spoils Me Rotten — and way too many more to list. If it's got a solid story, I've probably watched it or it's on my list.

Gaming

One of the things I can always fall back on. Whether it's getting lost in a story-driven game or just unwinding after a long day, gaming has always been a constant.

Music

Always there in the background. Music fills the silence and sets the mood for everything else I do. It's the one thing that ties all the other interests together.
